RBK Task
 - WebGL & Three.js

 	* TASK:
 		Design a game in WebGL and Three.js that has a cube moving on keyboardkeys clicks.

 	* MVP:
	 	-Design a Cube (6 faces, 6 colors/shades)
	 	-The cube should be sitting on a black floor/table with three white wallssurrounded (feel free to change the colors)
	 	-The cube moves (one step / one face) on keys press:
		 	-Press D to move right
		 	-Press A to move left
		 	-Press W to move up (toward the screen  if 3D)
		 	-Press S to move down (toward the player if 3D)
